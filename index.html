How Do WNBA Players Age? (Part I)

October 2025

An analysis of player aging curves and productivity changes throughout WNBA careers. Understanding when and how WNBA players peak and decline is crucial for roster management and player development decisions.

Key Findings:

  • WNBA players peak from ages 23-27
  • Players improve by 0.5 WS from age 22 to peak at 25
  • Decline begins at age 25, with larger and more consistent decline starting from age 28
  • Steep decline of 0.8 WS from ages 28-31

Research Highlights:

  • Win Share (WS) data from 1997-2025
  • Age-adjusted productivity analysis
  • Per-possession efficiency metrics
