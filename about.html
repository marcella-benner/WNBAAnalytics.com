About the Author

Lars Benner

PhD Graduate, Johns Hopkins University

Welcome to WNBAAnalytics.com! I'm Lars Benner, a recent PhD graduate from Johns Hopkins University with a passion for sports analytics and statistical modeling. My research focuses on developing advanced metrics to better understand player performance and team dynamics in women's basketball.

Through this platform, I share my analytical insights, research findings, and statistical models that aim to provide deeper understanding of the WNBA. My work combines rigorous statistical analysis with practical applications to help fans, coaches, and analysts better appreciate the nuances of the game.

Mission & Vision

My goal is to bridge the gap between academic research and practical sports analysis. The WNBA deserves the same level of analytical rigor that has been applied to men's basketball, and I'm committed to advancing the field of women's basketball analytics.

Innovation

Developing new metrics and analytical frameworks that provide fresh perspectives on player evaluation and team performance.

Accessibility

Making complex statistical concepts understandable and actionable for fans, coaches, and analysts at all levels.

Accuracy

Ensuring that all analyses are grounded in rigorous statistical methodology and validated against real-world outcomes.
