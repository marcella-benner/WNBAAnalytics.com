Win Contribution Data

Comprehensive dataset of WNBA player statistics and Win Contribution calculations

This dataset contains Win Contribution (WC) calculations for WNBA players across all seasons, using data provided by wnba.com and basketball-reference.com. The data includes traditional statistics as well as advanced metrics like Win Shares and our custom Win Contribution calculations.

Data Columns

  • Player: Player name
  • Season: Season year
  • AGE: Player age
  • MIN: Minutes played
  • PTS, FGM, FGA, 3PM, 3PA, FTM, FTA: Traditional scoring statistics
  • OREB, DREB: Offensive and defensive rebounds
  • AST, TOV, STL, BLK, PF: Assists, turnovers, steals, blocks, personal fouls
  • OFFRTG, DEFRTG: Offensive and defensive ratings
  • WS: Win Shares
  • win_score_final: Total Win Contribution
  • wins_per_100_poss: Win Contribution per 100 possessions
  • Pos: Position (G=Guard, F=Forward, C=Center)
